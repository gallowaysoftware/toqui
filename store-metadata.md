## Long Description

Toqui is your AI-powered travel companion that helps you plan smarter trips from start to finish. Whether you are exploring a new city or organizing a multi-stop adventure, Toqui connects you with over 800 expert AI personas -- from local food guides to adventure travel specialists -- to give you personalized recommendations tailored to your travel style.

Key features:

- AI Chat Planning: Have natural conversations to build your perfect itinerary. Ask about restaurants, attractions, hidden gems, and logistics.
- Expert Personas: Get advice from specialized AI travel experts -- sommeliers, hiking guides, history buffs, budget travel pros, and more.
- Smart Itineraries: Automatically generated day-by-day itineraries with maps, times, and details you can export to PDF or calendar.
- Booking Integration: Find and book hotels, flights, and experiences directly through curated recommendations.
- Trip Sharing: Share your trip plans with travel companions via a simple link.
- Multi-Trip Management: Organize all your upcoming and past trips in one place.
- Export Anywhere: Download your itinerary as a PDF or add events to your calendar with one tap.

Toqui is built for travelers who want more than a generic list of tourist spots. Get the kind of advice you would expect from a well-traveled friend who knows the destination inside and out.
