### Description (4000 chars max)

Plan smarter trips in minutes, not hours. Toqui is your AI travel companion with 800+ expert personas -- local food critics, adventure guides, history buffs, budget travel pros -- who help you build personalized itineraries through natural conversation.

Just describe your trip and start chatting. Toqui builds your day-by-day itinerary as you talk -- no forms, no templates, no guesswork.

WHY TRAVELERS CHOOSE TOQUI

-- 800+ Expert Personas --
Not just one generic AI. Get advice from a Tokyo street food guide, a Tuscany wine expert, a Southeast Asia backpacking pro, or a Paris nightlife specialist. Each persona brings deep knowledge of their destination and specialty.

-- Smart Itineraries with Maps --
Your conversations become organized day-by-day plans with times, locations, and an interactive map. See your entire trip laid out and adjust on the fly.

-- Real-Time Travel Companion --
Toqui works during your trip, not just before it. Restaurant closed? Get an alternative in seconds. Flight delayed? Adjust your itinerary instantly. Need directions or a translation? Just ask.

-- Booking Management --
Forward confirmation emails and Toqui automatically extracts flight, hotel, and activity details into your trip. Everything in one place.

-- Group Trip Planning --
Share your trip with travel companions via a simple link. Everyone contributes ideas, and the AI synthesizes all preferences into one itinerary that works for the whole group.

-- Export Anywhere --
Download your itinerary as a PDF or add events to your calendar with one tap. Share a beautiful read-only link with anyone.

-- Weather Forecasts --
See real-time weather data for your destination so you can plan outdoor activities with confidence.

PRICING THAT RESPECTS TRAVELERS

Free to start -- your first trip includes a Pro trial with full access to all features. After that:

- Trip Pro: $19 CAD per trip (one-time, not recurring)
- Explorer: $9.99 CAD/month -- 3 active trips, unlimited messages, all personas
- Voyager: $17.99 CAD/month -- unlimited trips, priority AI responses

No subscriptions you forget about. No affiliate-driven recommendations. Toqui recommends what is best for you, not what pays us the most.

PRIVACY FIRST

Toqui is built by a Canadian company and complies with GDPR and PIPEDA. We do not sell your data, serve tracking ads, or let affiliate revenue influence recommendations. Your travel plans stay private.

## Screenshot Descriptions

Five screenshots should be captured for both App Store and Play Store listings. Each should use device frames and a short headline overlay.

### Screenshot 1: AI Chat Planning
Headline: "Plan Any Trip in Minutes"
Show the chat screen mid-conversation where the user has described a trip (e.g., "10 days in Japan") and the AI persona (a local food guide) is responding with specific restaurant and neighborhood recommendations. The persona name and avatar should be visible at the top. This establishes the core product experience immediately -- conversational trip planning with specialized expertise.

### Screenshot 2: Smart Itinerary with Map
Headline: "Your Itinerary, Built from Conversation"
Show the trip overview screen with a day-by-day itinerary timeline on the left and an interactive map with pins on the right (or stacked on mobile). Include 3-4 days of planned activities with times and locations visible. This demonstrates the tangible output -- conversations become real, organized travel plans.

### Screenshot 3: Expert Persona Selection
Headline: "800+ Expert Travel Personas"
Show the persona switching interface or a moment where the AI introduces itself as a specialist (e.g., "I'm a Tuscany wine and food expert"). Include visible persona categories or a grid of persona avatars with their specialties. This differentiates Toqui from generic AI chatbots and showcases the depth of expertise available.

### Screenshot 4: Booking Management
Headline: "All Your Bookings in One Place"
Show the bookings screen with 2-3 imported bookings (a flight, a hotel, and an activity) displaying extracted details like dates, confirmation numbers, and locations. Include the email forwarding card explaining the automatic import feature. This demonstrates practical travel utility beyond just planning.

### Screenshot 5: Trip Sharing and Collaboration
Headline: "Plan Together, Travel Together"
Show the shared trip view or the collaborator invite screen with multiple collaborators listed. Include the share link interface and the collaborator roles (owner, editor, viewer). This highlights the group trip planning feature, which is inherently viral and a key differentiator.

### Keyword Strategy Notes

- The iOS keyword field (100 chars) should prioritize terms NOT already in the title or subtitle, since Apple indexes those separately. The keyword field above excludes "Toqui," "companion," "plan," and "expert" which are already covered.
- Google Play indexes the full description, so all target keywords are naturally woven into the description text. No separate keyword field exists.
- Monitor rankings weekly after launch and rotate low-performing keywords out of the iOS field. The promotional text field (170 chars) can be updated without a new build and should be refreshed seasonally (e.g., "Plan your summer trip" in May, "Plan your holiday trip" in November).
- Localized keyword sets for French Canadian (fr-CA) should be prepared before expanding to Quebec market.
