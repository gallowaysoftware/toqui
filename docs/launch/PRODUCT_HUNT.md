## 3. Maker Comment (Launch Day)

Hey Product Hunt -- I'm the solo founder behind Toqui.

I built this because I kept paying for AI travel tools I used twice a year. $10/month for Layla, $20/month for ChatGPT Plus, and every "free" planner was just a funnel to push overpriced hotel bookings at me. I was paying $240/year to plan two trips.

So I built something with a different premise: you should only pay when you actually have a trip to plan. Toqui costs $19 per trip. That's it. No recurring charges sneaking through your credit card statement six months after your vacation.

What makes it different beyond pricing: Toqui has 800+ specialized AI personas. When you're planning wine country in Tuscany, you're talking to a sommelier persona. Hiking in Patagonia? A trail guide who knows the weather windows. Bangkok street food? A local food critic. These aren't gimmicks -- they're trained on domain-specific knowledge and they give meaningfully better recommendations than a generic chatbot.

The part I'm most proud of: Toqui works during your trip, not just before. Restaurant closed? Get a replacement in 10 seconds. Flight delayed? Your itinerary adjusts. Weather turned? The AI re-plans your afternoon.

We're also privacy-first by default -- GDPR baseline for every user regardless of location, no tracking, no data selling. Canadian company, Canadian values.

Your first trip is free for PH users today. Try planning a real upcoming trip and let me know what you think.
